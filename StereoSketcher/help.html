Pro tips

  • To crop your image before uploading, just resize your browser window.

Other help

  • This was tested in Firefox during development, results in other browsers may vary.
  • Laptop screens are unkind to the red/cyan images.
  • Please note that the grayish polarized 3D glasses you get from move theaters nowadays will not work for StereoSketcher.
  • Some colors work better than others in red/cyan mode. i.e. red doesn't work well.
  • If you are getting the images to line up in parallel or cross viewing, but the image is weird or even 3D in the wrong direction, you might be using the wrong view mode - try switching view modes. Also have a look at this image:
